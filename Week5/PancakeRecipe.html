Good Old Fashioned Pancakes

Ingredients

  1. 1 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  2. 3 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
  3. 1 teaspoon sugar
  4. 1 teaspoon salt
  5. 1 1/4 cups of milk
  6. 1 egg
  7. 1 teaspoon melted butter

Steps

Step 1

In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.

Step 2

Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour the batter onto the griddle using around 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
